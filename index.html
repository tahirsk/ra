About

Sukha Dukha Media

Sukha Dukha Media started from a very personal place. Our founder M Dattathreya had always been deeply passionate about storytelling and visual craft. Long before the idea of starting a company ever felt real, he was already creating content on his own, figuring out what made a brand feel alive and what made people actually stop and pay attention.

Since then the journey has been nothing short of transformative. For Dattathreya personally it has been a process of growth, learning and showing up every single day with full commitment. For the clients who have worked with us it has meant real results, sharper identities and a creative partner they could actually trust. We have worked with brands across categories, built campaigns that performed, and created content that people genuinely remembered.

Today Sukha Dukha Media is a Bangalore based creative studio built around one idea: that great work comes from people who care. We combine strategy, production and digital execution not because it sounds good on paper but because we have seen firsthand that it is the only way to build a brand that lasts.

The Space

At Sukha Dukha Media, we offer a versatile studio rental space in Bangalore designed for creators, brands, and businesses who want results driven visuals. Our studio is built to support product shoots, model shoots, UGC content, reels, ads, and brand campaigns.

The space is thoughtfully designed and can be adapted to suit different shoot requirements, from clean product setups to lifestyle and creator focused content. We also provide high end professional equipment on rental, ensuring you have access to premium lighting and tools without the hassle of ownership.

Whether you're a brand, content creator, or production team, our studio empowers you to execute sharp, high quality visuals that elevate your story and make your content stand out.
